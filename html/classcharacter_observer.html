Detailed Description

Character observer class, implementing the observer pattern. It also inherits the QMainWindow class of the QT framework. This class generates a QT table widget, where the character's stats are displayed. In the final application, this will be used by the player to monitor his character's or the npc's stats.

The QT framework was chosen because of its simplicity and good documentation
